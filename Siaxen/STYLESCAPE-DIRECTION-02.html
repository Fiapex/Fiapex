WHY THIS DIRECTION
01
Teal, not blue
Medical blue is the most overused color in healthcare. Teal sits in a different space entirely: deeper, calmer, more intellectual. It signals analytical thinking without the clinical coldness that every MedTech competitor defaults to.
02
Convergence, not construction
Direction 01 expresses “replacing fragmentation with architecture” through straight lines becoming grids. This direction expresses the same promise through scattered paths being engineered into one convergent system. Different visual language, identical meaning.
03
Parametric, not decorative
Every curve follows a calculated path. This serves the Sage archetype (30% of the brand personality): intellectual depth, calculated precision, and quiet confidence in the face of complexity.
